About East West Group

East West Group is a leading provider of comprehensive solutions in heavy machinery and equipment, headquartered in Dubai, UAE. With years of industry expertise, we specialize in delivering innovative, reliable, and sustainable solutions tailored to meet the needs of the mining, construction, and agricultural sectors.

Our Vision

At East West Group, we envision a future where advanced technology and sustainable practices redefine the possibilities in the mining, construction, and agricultural sectors. We strive to be the partner of choice for our customers by consistently delivering value, fostering innovation, and building long-term relationships. Our vision is to contribute to global progress by supporting the development of essential infrastructure and sustainable resource management.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower industries with cutting-edge equipment and unparalleled services that drive efficiency and productivity. We aim to set the benchmark in the heavy machinery industry through customer-focused solutions, innovation, and unwavering commitment to excellence. By prioritizing safety, sustainability, and operational excellence, we aspire to help our clients achieve their goals seamlessly.
